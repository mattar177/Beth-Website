INNOCENT NOT IGNORANT

A campaign to make parents, carers, and teachers aware of age inapropriate sex and relationship education resources. Every child's innocence needs to be protected to keep them mentally and physically safe while growing up. We protect our children from so many things, many of which aren't nearly as harmful as premature sexual exposure. This campaign is promoting awareness and action against indecent resources in schools. Children can be protected against sexual abuse without being informed and encouraged in sexual behaviour, as proven through resources like 'talk PANTS'.
